### Test #2: Page Meaning

1. Activate a **screen reader** and browse through the page content **systematically** from top to bottom. Are both of the following statements true? If not, raise an issue (1.3.2, 1.3.2, 1.3.2).
   
   - The screen reader encounters all static content in a **correct reading sequence**[1](#footnote-page-meaning-1).
   - The screen reader does *not* encounter any static content that is **meant to be hidden** from users.

[\[1\]](#footnote-trigger-page-meaning-1): A **correct reading sequence** is "any sequence where words and paragraphs are presented in an order that does not change the meaning of the content." Source: [*WCAG 2.1 Glossary*](https://www.w3.org/TR/WCAG21/#dfn-correct-reading-sequence).
